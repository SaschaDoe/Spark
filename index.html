The Story

Act I: When The Lights Dim

As Finn of the Sun People helplessly watches his suns die out, Nova of the Deep Folk hunts for the life-giving "Spark" to save her cousin Tam. However, neither can solve their problem alone. For that, they must recognize the message in the "Writing on the Wall," which shows them a single path forward that can only be walked together.

Act II: The Underground Adventure

They venture into the "Wild Lands" where they meet Luna, the only one who can guide them into the desert. Their journey is not only to shake off the Sheriff who is pursuing them to protect them (while also keeping them from their destiny), but also to find Tam. Their hopes falter when they realize that all their efforts might be for nothing. They must go further still, into the heart of an ancient, world-spanning secret.

Act III: Healing The Sun

The Sheriff finds his true destiny, and together they board a magical train that carries them to the heart of evil. There, united for the final battle, they must give their all to defeat what remains when duty outlives its makers.
