The Story

Act I: When The Lights Dim

The magical sun starts flickering. Finn (Sun Folk) and Nova (Deep Folk) must overcome their families' ancient rivalry to create the sparks needed to heal their dying world.

Act II: The Underground Adventure

Trapped underground, Finn and Nova discover the truth about their kingdom. With new allies Luna, Zephyr, and Old Pete, they journey through forgotten places and uncover ancient secrets.

Act III: Healing The Sun

The final battle against Lady Seraphina tests everyone's courage. Heroes make sacrifices, the sun is healed, but victory comes with a price that changes everyone forever.
